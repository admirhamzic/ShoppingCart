# Shopping Cart

Simple and basic shopping cart program that prints a receipt of the items in your cart and the total amount of said items combined

Output example:


```java
+-------------------------+
|       JAVA MARKET       |
+-----------------+-------+
|     Product     | Price |
+-----------------+-------+
| Milk            | 20,54 |
| Butter          | 10,34 |
| Cookies         | 10,34 |
+-----------------+-------+
|      TOTAL      | 41.22 |
+-----------------+-------+
```
